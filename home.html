What we offer

Everything you need
to thrive at university.

WellMind brings together mood tracking, expert resources, and real human support — designed specifically for the USIU student experience.

🧠

Mood Check-In

A 3-minute guided check-in assesses your emotional state, stress, sleep, and social wellbeing — giving you a personal wellness score each time.

📈

Progress Tracking

See your wellness trends over time. Celebrate good weeks and spot patterns before they become problems — all in a private dashboard.

💬

Counsellor Connect

Book a confidential session with a USIU-trained counsellor directly from the app. Walk-in and virtual options available every week.

🌙

Sleep & Stress Tools

Guided breathing exercises, sleep hygiene tips, and personalised stress-management strategies tailored to your check-in responses.

🤝

Peer Support Groups

Connect with fellow USIU students in facilitated peer circles. Share experiences, build friendships, and reduce the weight of loneliness.

📚

Academic Integration

Linked to the Academic Skills Centre — get study coaching, exam prep, and time management support when stress is affecting your performance.

How it works

Simple steps,
real impact.

No long forms, no stigma, no judgement. WellMind meets you exactly where you are.

1
Create your account
Sign up with your USIU email or Google in under 60 seconds. Completely free for all registered students.
2
Do your first check-in
Answer 6 simple questions about your mood, stress, sleep, and social life. Takes about 3 minutes.
3
Get your wellness score
Receive a personalised score and tailored insights — what's going well and where you might need a little support.
4
Access the right support
From self-help resources to booking a counsellor — WellMind guides you to exactly what you need, when you need it.
